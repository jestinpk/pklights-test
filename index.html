About PK Lights

PK Lights is a dedicated B2B wholesale supplier specialising in LED lighting and electrical products. We cater exclusively to shops, retailers, contractors, and project buyers across South India.

Our focus is on bulk supply — ensuring you get the right products at competitive wholesale prices, dispatched quickly so your business never slows down.

  • 🏪Bulk supply for retail shops & electrical stores
  • 🏗️Project-scale orders for contractors & builders
  • 💡Wide range from basic LED bulbs to programmable lighting
  • 🚚Reliable daily dispatch for confirmed orders
  • 🌏Serving South India with consistent quality
